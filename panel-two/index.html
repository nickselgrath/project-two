Which Porsche Is For You?

Choose from Porsche's latest line up of both gas and electric sports cars

I'm Ready

German Engineering At Its Finest

Porsche creates vehicles for every need. From sedans to coupes to SUVs, they have you covered for whatever you could desire. However, the underlying DNA that makes a Porsche a Porsche is the unirvaled performance on the track and the street. Read about some of Porsche's top sports car models to choose which one you would like to see in your garage.

Porsche 911 GT3

The new 992 generation 911 GT3 is unrivaled in terms of driver experience and performance for money. The brand new double-wishbone front suspension up front connects the driver directly with the asphalt beneath and the roar of the 9000 RPM redline makes for a raw and exciting driving experience.

Specs

  • Drivetrain: 4.0L Flat-Six
  • Horsepower: 503
  • Torque: 347 lb/ft
  • Weight: 3164 lbs
  • Transmission: 6-Speed Manual / 7-Speed PDK
  • 0-60 MPH: 3.4 Seconds (PDK) / 3.9 Seconds (Manual)
  • Nürburgring Lap Time: 6:55.2

Price

  • MSRP: $171,150
  • Market Price: >$200,000

Porsche 911 Turbo S

The new 992 generation 911 Turbo S is a sleek, stylish, and powerful grand tourer capable of immense comfort at speed. It's focused on daily drivability with a sporty influence, and boy is that influence strong as the Turbo S's AWD system makes it the fastest accelerating car in the lineup.

Specs

  • Drivetrain: 3.8L Twin-Turbo Flat-Six
  • Horsepower: 640
  • Torque: 590 lb/ft
  • Weight: 3636 lbs
  • Transmission: 7-Speed PDK
  • 0-60 MPH: 2.6 Seconds
  • Nürburgring Lap Time: 7:17.3

Price

  • MSRP: $216,100
  • Market Price: >$250,000

Porsche Taycan Turbo S

The new flagship electric sedan is an absolute powerhouse with enough room for your whole family and some luggage. It's not just limited to straight-line speed though, as the Porsche engineers have worked their suspension magic to make a heavy electric car feel as nimble as a porsche should.

Specs

  • Drivetrain: Three Electric Motors
  • Horsepower: 750
  • Torque: 770 lb/ft
  • Weight: 5101 lbs
  • Transmission: 2-Speed Automatic
  • 0-60 MPH: 2.6 Seconds
  • Nürburgring Lap Time: 7:33.0

Price

  • MSRP: $187,400
  • Market Price: $140,000-$170,000
